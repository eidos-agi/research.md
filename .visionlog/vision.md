research.md is an MCP server that enforces structured research workflows through tooling, not convention. It exists because AI agents will always take the path of least resistance — if a tool allows shallow work, agents will produce shallow work.

The north star: **make shallow research structurally impossible and deep research the only path forward.**

Every evidence grade must be earned. Every finding must prove the researcher engaged with primary material. Every decision must trace back to validated evidence. The tool is opinionated about rigor — it refuses work that doesn't meet the bar, rather than accepting everything and hoping for the best.

research.md is one leg of the Eidos decision trilogy:
- **research.md**: earn decisions with evidence
- **visionlog**: record decisions as contracts
- **ike.md**: execute work within those contracts

The flow is one-way. A decision skipped in research.md is a contract that was never earned.
